A bit about me

I'm Alex — a designer-developer hybrid who's been building things for the web for over six years. Started in graphic design, fell in love with code, and never really separated the two.

I work with startups and creative studios to design and build products that feel considered — the kind of software that just feels good to use.

When I'm not at the computer, you'll find me at a coffee shop, shooting on film, or endlessly reorganising my Notion workspace.
